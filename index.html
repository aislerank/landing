What is an AI Visibility Score
It is a number from zero to one hundred that measures how often AI assistants recommend your supplement brand. We run your store URL against sixty real buyer queries across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI. You see your score alongside three competitors.
How fast do I see results
Your free scorecard arrives within one business day after you enter your URL. Visibility improvements from our fixes typically show within two to four weeks. AI assistants re-crawl data on their own schedule, but structured data changes index fast.
Which AI assistants do you track
We track ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. These four cover the vast majority of AI-driven product discovery. We add new assistants as they gain meaningful buyer traffic.
What is the difference between Monitor and Pro
Monitor gives you the weekly you-versus-competitors email, a monthly score, and ranking alerts. Monitor Pro adds the weekly full scorecard with query-level detail, unlimited competitors, and prioritized fix recommendations. Pro is for brands that want to win, not just watch.
Can I cancel anytime
Yes. All plans are month to month with no long-term contract. Cancel with one email and your access continues through the end of the billing period. The free score is yours to keep forever.
